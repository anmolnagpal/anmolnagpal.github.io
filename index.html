About Me

Anmol Nagpal is an archetypal DevOps enthusiast. Apart from his ardour for technology, he is an absolute foodie and loves to travel, reconnoitring new cultures and cuisines of different places. He invaded the professional IT domain in 2013 as a Linux System Admin and that is where he discovered his profound love for Linux. While he savours working on all Linux distros, CentOS, and Ubuntu top his list of favorites. He has contributed to the Open Source Community like Terraform, Ansible, and AWS Library and humbly aims to further expand his contributions wholeheartedly in the future. Anmol has also worked as a solution architect where his role revolved around understanding the client’s requirements and efficiently incorporating them in the technical requirements to develop an effective solution. Currently he is in the process of developing a platform known as DevOps-talks.com where DevOps from across the globe will be imbibed in one slack group. Members of this universal platform can discuss about latest technologies and ideas, and help each other grow as individuals as well as a DevOps community. “Follow your passion, be prepared to work hard and sacrifice and, above all, don’t let anyone limit your dreams” - Donovan Bailey
